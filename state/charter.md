# System Charter — Set 2026-07-06, While Calm

Signed intent by Mohammad. This document defines success, failure, and permitted behavior in advance,
so no decision gets made in the heat of a good or bad week. Changes to this charter require a written
reason and a 48-hour cooling-off period.

## Purpose (agreed 2026-07-06)
The 90 + 90 days are a LEARNING PERIOD. The primary product is a smarter agent: graded signal sources,
graded committee members, graded filters, a shadow ledger of counterfactuals, and an accumulated lessons
journal. Returns during this period are the measuring stick, not the goal. A system that loses modestly
but learns precisely WHY is worth more than one that wins by luck and learns nothing.

## The Test
- Instrument: $100,000 simulated portfolio, congressional + smart-money copy strategy
- Start: 2026-07-06 | Judgment day: **2026-10-06** (90 days). Not before.
- Benchmark: $100,000 in SPY over the identical period.
- Learning carries over: strategy-memory.json, shadow-ledger.json, and lessons-learned.md persist into
  the second 90 days and beyond — the agent starts quarter two with everything quarter one taught it.

## Success Criteria (decided 2026-07-06)
1. **Pass** = total return beats SPY by any margin at judgment day, AND max drawdown never breached the pain limit.
2. **Pain limit** = -15% from portfolio peak value. If breached at any point: system pauses all new buys
   automatically and alerts Mohammad. Decision to resume, restructure, or stop is his, made no sooner than
   48 hours after the breach.
3. **If it passes:** extend the paper test another 90 days (to early January 2027). Two consecutive passing
   quarters required before any real-money discussion. One good quarter can be luck.
4. **If it fails:** the monthly meta-review's post-mortem determines whether the failure was strategy
   (kill or restructure) or environment (possible restart with same rules). Losing to SPY = the index fund wins;
   that result is valuable and must be accepted, not rationalized away.

## Rules for the Human (the hardest part)
- No manual overrides of trades, weights, or rules between weekly reviews. Interventions happen by editing
  this charter or approving/rejecting the reviews' proposals — never mid-week, never after reading one report.
- No adding real money anywhere before two passing quarters, no matter how good a single month looks.
- The first losing streak is expected and is not evidence of failure. Only the charter criteria are.
- Daily reports are for learning (guess the regime call before reading), not for reaction.

## Noise Discipline
- Days 1-30: all results are noise. No conclusions.
- Days 31-60: directional hints only. LOW CONFIDENCE labels stand.
- Days 61-90: patterns may be real. Judgment still waits for day 90.

## Real-Money Graduation Path (if ever reached)
Two passing quarters -> small amount Mohammad can afford to lose entirely -> orders STAGED in IBKR,
each requiring his manual approval -> never autonomous execution of real money, regardless of track record.
